About · Our Initiative

AI you can understand, and therefore trust

CARAI exists to make artificial intelligence explainable by design — advancing the fundamentals while pursuing wide-reaching, human-centered applications.

Our mission

Explainable AI provides reliable decision assistance for humans — pursuing the future of AI by developing its fundamentals while seeking advanced, wide-reaching applications.

What we stand for
Transparency

Every prediction should come with a faithful, human-readable account of why.

Trustworthiness

Robust, calibrated models that behave reliably under real-world shift.

Human-centered

AI measured by the good it does for the people who rely on it.

Impact

Foundational theory translated into deployable, wide-reaching applications.
