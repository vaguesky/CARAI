01

Reliable decision assistance for people

Explainable AI provides reliable decision assistance for humans in human–AI interaction. Developing robust and trustworthy AI models is now essential for leading-edge AI technology R&D.

CARAI pursues the future of AI by developing its fundamentals while seeking advanced and wide-reaching applications across four coordinated research groups.
